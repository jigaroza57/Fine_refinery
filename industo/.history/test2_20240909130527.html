    LEADERS PAR EXCELLENCE

    Mohammed Moquim

    Managing Director | METRO GROUP Founder | Founder of MOQUIM FOUNDATION

    A Moderator with an Imagination

    Nothing is Impossible, provided you have the determination and burning Passion to chase your Dreams. Mohammed Moquim is a leader in the real estate sector who has been working towards transforming the state of Odisha since 1993. With almost 30 years of experience across various disciplines, Moquim has been dedicated to contributing to the state's development. As the head of the Metro Group, he has led a construction and real estate revolution in Odisha, resulting in the company becoming a significant business conglomerate with interests in multiple sectors. Moquim's unwavering focus on excellence and innovation is evident in all of the projects undertaken by the Metro Group. In addition, he has held leadership positions in various real estate associations, including serving as the former President of CREDAI (Orissa) and Joint Secretary of CREDAI National.

    Mr. Peeyush Dhari Mohanty

    Director - Metro Group | Presidency Hotels and Resorts, Metro Guards Pvt. Ltd. | Co-Founder of the Iconic Brand Metro Group

    Passionate Leader Driving Success in Business

    Leading by example is one of the most important virtues of a leader, and this is a mantra that Peeyush Dhari Mohanty has followed throughout his career. If he wants his team to be passionate, committed, dedicated, and motivated, he believes that he must set the example himself. He has a rare blend of education from various international schools and a degree in mechanical engineering. His interest in human organizations at all levels, including social, political, and economic, has driven his personal calling in business. He is a leader and pathfinder who has the confidence to stand alone, make tough decisions, and show compassion for others. He started his journey as the co-founder of Metro Group, which has landmark buildings in Bhubaneswar and Cuttack and has since established a conglomerate of niche companies, including The Presidency Hotel and Resorts. He strives to empower people through employment in all sectors of society and believes that being different is crucial for success in entrepreneurship and leadership. With gumption and unbridled enthusiasm, he is committed to making a positive impact on society and raising the bar.

    Sofia Firdous

    Director - Metro Group
    Ms. Sofia Firdous is the Director of Metro Group and has a degree in Leadership Management from Leadership Management International in the USA. She is a next-generation entrepreneur who has been leading the business to new heights. Ms. Sofia is responsible for multiple aspects of the business including operations, management, marketing, and overall growth. She is dedicated to her work and plays a key role in the Business Development and Corporate Communications of the company. Additionally, Sofia serves as the President of Bhubaneswar CREDAI for the years 2023-25. She has become the first woman to lead a city or a state chapter in CREDAI. She is also leading the Skill Committee and is the CWW East Zone Coordinator at CREDAI, from 2022 to 2025. She holds other diverse roles, including membership in CREDAI's National Skill, Sustainable & RERA Committee, Co-Chair at CII-Indian Green Building Council (IGBC) Bhubaneswar Chapter, and membership in CII, IWN Odisha. She is a Core Committee Member of ICC and IWEC Odisha, alongside being a Member of FICCI FLO Odisha. She also played an important role in organizing Indias First Womens Business Mela at Bhubaneswar in 2022, which was a huge success.

    MD Aleem

    Director - Metro Group
    With over a decade of seasoned experience in the real estate industry, Mohammad Aleem is a driving force behind Metro Group's success. Having excelled in the management of Metro Group's hospitality arm, Aleem brings strategic leadership and dynamism to his current role as he takes care of Business Development & PR. Beyond his professional commitments, Mohammed is passionately dedicated to social upliftment and actively engages in various impactful social initiatives. He is the trustee of the Moquim Foundation.
